Homepage

Your home page needs to load fast, and immediately provide the right information to your users. It will have your organizations color scheme, a modern design, and the calls to action needed for users to engage with the website for example a sign up for your newsletter section. It will also have the full menu of your website, with all pages at a maximum of three clicks away.

Who We Are

This page is vital to create a connection with as its often the first stop for a new visitor. In it, visitors will learn who you are, what you do, and how you do it. This page may be a deciding factor before you convince a visitor to:

  • Downloading a piece of content.
  • Signing up for your newsletter.
  • Requesting information from the organization.

What We Do

This page is vital to illustrate to visitors the mission and vision of the organization. The page will clearly show the successes of the organization.

Portal

Using Google's acclaimed firebase authentication, we can create safe and secure portals that identity and authorises users. Knowing a user's identity allows the app to securely save user data in the cloud and provide the same personalized experience across all of the user's devices.

Donate

Integration of various online payment processing tools such as stripe and mobile platforms such as Mpesa will connect your organization to donors in nearly every country in the world and ensure access to the funding and support you need to serve the community.

Database and Storage

We will integrate Google's Firebase Database, a cloud-hosted database. Data is stored as JSON and synchronized in realtime to every connected client. The service will be cross-platform with our iOS, Android. All of the clients will share one Realtime Database instance and automatically receive updates with the newest data.

Separation of Administrator and User Accounts

No normal user accounts should have Administrator access to your network. Users that have Administrator access as part of their normal user account could inadvertently cause a lot of damage if (for example) they are infected by a virus that deletes data. If a user requires special permissions, they should be given details of an Administrator account that has the required level of access.
